How this works (rules)
  • Overdue — past 1.5× your usual cadence with this friend (e.g. you meet every 10 wks, last seen 16 wks ago). Red left border.
  • Drifting — past 1.75× cadence AND at least 12 weeks gap. Amber pill next to name. Friends drift here when meets get noticeably late.
  • Mostly group — fewer than 20% of meets with them were 1-on-1. Group-section tracks them; individual overdue only fires when a real solo pattern is being neglected.
  • Group buddy — 20–50% of meets were solo. Mixed signal.
  • Auto-hidden — no contact at all in 30+ months. Drops from feed silently. (Mark them dormant manually if you want them out sooner.)
  • Closeness weighting — friends you've met more in the last 12 months rank higher in the list. Doesn't change the overdue flag, just the order.
  • Cadence — target_cadence_weeks on the Friends tab is authoritative when filled in (any number, including 52). Leave the cell empty to use the learned cadence from your meet history (last 36 months when possible, else all-time).
